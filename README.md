# 🎸 Blues Burger — Old North, Tel Aviv

## An American Blues Diner. Reborn in Tel Aviv.

**Blues Burger** is a blues-themed American diner concept for Old North, Tel Aviv — premium cheeseburgers, live blues music, vintage vinyl, red leather booths, and neon everywhere.

This repository contains the complete investor pitch package — interactive websites, 3D walkthroughs, interior design concepts, and packaging design.

---

## 📁 What's Inside

### `/website/index.html` — Main Landing Page
The full animated Blues Burger website with stage curtain reveal, floating music notes, animated equalizer bars, mouse-following spotlight, custom cursor, and the iconic hanging comic-book neon sign.

### `/street-3d/street-view.html` — 3D Street Scene
A full city block at night built in Three.js. Blues Burger's storefront blazes with neon while ugly neighboring shops stay dark. Features a **massive spinning rooftop neon sign** with Vegas-style gold scrollwork, chase lights, and multi-color glow. Parked cars, street lamps, trees, and sidewalk details.

### `/interior-3d/restaurant-hd.html` — 3D Interior (HD)
The complete restaurant rendered in high-definition Three.js with procedural brick/wood/tile textures, physically correct lighting, neon tube geometry with glow halos, smoke particles, and exposed ductwork. Includes all zones: booths, bourbon bar, corner stage, guitar wall, open kitchen, and vinyl corner.

### `/interior-3d/full-floorplan.html` — 3D Full Floorplan
The entire 18m × 12m restaurant in one unified 3D space with 7 switchable camera angles and smooth cinematic transitions.

### `/care-package/unboxing-3d.html` — 3D Care Package Unboxing
Interactive 3D product showcase. Click "Open It" to watch each item reveal its contents:
- **Vinyl Burger Box** → lid lifts, cheeseburger rises out
- **Amp Fries Box** → golden fries blast out the speaker
- **Album Cover Sleeve** → slides open, vinyl record emerges
- **Guitar Pick** → spins out of paper sleeve

### `/care-package/showcase.html` — Care Package SVG Showcase
Detailed SVG product illustrations of all four care package items with descriptions and per-unit cost estimates.

### `/interior-design/` — Interior Concept Art
Three SVG-illustrated interior design concepts:
- `vegas-neon-concept.html` — Fremont Street inspired, ornate gold neon
- `gentleman-concept.html` — Premium gentleman's club aesthetic
- `full-concept.html` — Complete room-by-room illustrations

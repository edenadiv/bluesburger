The Vinyl Burger Box

Every burger ships in a custom round box shaped like a 12-inch vinyl record. Matte black exterior with embossed grooves you can feel. The center label carries the Blues Burger logo in red and gold neon print. Pop the lid and there's your burger, wrapped in wax paper with the BB stamp, sitting in a custom-fit cardboard cradle. The box is rigid enough to reuse — customers keep them as decoration, coasters, or wall art. Every delivery is an unboxing moment.

Cost estimate: ₪3.5–5 per unit at 5,000+ MOQ. Round corrugated cardboard with matte laminate + spot UV on the label. Food-safe wax paper interior liner.

The Amp Fries Box

Fries come in a miniature guitar amplifier. The box is a rectangular sleeve printed to look like a Marshall-style tube amp — control knobs (Volume, Tone, Blues), a speaker grille, corner screws, and a red power LED printed on the front. The fries stick out the open top like they're blasting out of the speaker. It's ridiculous. It's unforgettable. It's the thing people photograph and post before they eat a single fry.

Cost estimate: ₪1.5–2.5 per unit. Printed card stock sleeve, single fold assembly. Same footprint as a standard fry box — just better looking.
Amp-Shaped Sleeve Printed Knobs & Grille Open-Top for Fries Instagram-First Design Flat-Pack Shipping

The Album Cover Sleeve

The outer packaging for every takeaway order is a 12-inch album sleeve. The front is designed like a real vinyl album cover — "Blues Burger: The Old North Sessions" — with the ornate gold neon border, red script logo, green neon guitar, and a Spotify QR code. The back has the menu printed as a track listing. Side A: Burgers. Side B: Sides & Shakes. It's a menu, a brand piece, and a Spotify gateway in one. Customers keep these. They frame them. They post them.

Cost estimate: ₪2–3.5 per unit. 300gsm art card, matte laminate exterior, spot UV on neon elements. Flat pack, folds around the vinyl burger box.
12" Album Sleeve Spotify QR Code Menu as Track Listing Ornate Gold Border Frameable Art

The Guitar Pick

Every care package includes a custom Blues Burger guitar pick. Deep crimson cellulose, printed with the logo in red and gold. It's a 0.73mm medium — actually playable, not a toy. Musicians use them. Non-musicians keep them in their wallet, on their keychain, or stuck to the fridge. It costs almost nothing to produce and it stays with the customer forever. The ultimate tiny brand ambassador.

Cost estimate: ₪0.30–0.50 per unit at 10,000+ MOQ. Custom cellulose or Delrin. Full-color UV print both sides. Delivered in a small paper sleeve inside the album cover.
